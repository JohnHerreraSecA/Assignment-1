Hi there! I'm John. This page came to life in the summer of 2024 as part of a Web Programming course assignment. But it quickly turned into a place to share my travel adventures. Honestly, these trips have transformed me into an itinerary-planning, fun-seeking, travel-loving enthusiast!


Born amidst the vibrant sprawl of Los Angeles, with its palm-lined streets and kaleidoscope of cultures, I was immersed in a tapestry of diversity from the very beginning. Yet, it was the move to Toronto during my formative years that truly shaped my identity. Nestled within the welcoming embrace of Canada's largest city, I discovered a new rhythm of life amidst its bustling streets and friendly neighborhoods. The juxtaposition of my birthplace's sun-soaked energy with Toronto's multicultural mosaic instilled in me a deep appreciation for both the dynamic allure of the West Coast and the inclusive warmth of the Great White North. From the palm trees of LA to the maple leaves of Toronto, my upbringing straddled two worlds, enriching my perspective and fueling my curiosity about the rich tapestry of human experience.


These days, I'm diving deep into the world of academia, navigating the intricate pathways of computer programming within the dynamic landscape of college life. From mastering the fundamentals of algorithms and data structures to exploring the latest technologies shaping our digital future, every lecture and lab session offers a glimpse into the vast possibilities of software development. This journey is more than just about learning to code; it's about understanding the potential to create, innovate, and transform the world around us through technology.
